What We Do

SP primary tuition is a specialist primary tuition administered by QTS qualified teachers. All our fully qualified teachers are bespoked pedagoy to ensure all our children reach their maximum academic potential

We specialise in 11 plus tutoring and SATs preparations. However, we beleive it is crucial to build a solid foundation in all aspects of primary learning to ensure children remain lifelong learners. The sooner we can start building your child's foundation the geater the results

How We Achieve Results:

Our specialist pedagogies are tailored to primary school children. We believe young children understand abstract concepts best through the use of visual representations and physical objects.

Our teachers provide creative learning activities to ensure children not only understand new concepts but also retain the concept. We then combine our methodology with structured learning programmes tailored to your child needs and aspirations. All our programmes are administered by teachers who are of Qualified Teacher Status (QTS) to ensure your child receives the best support.
